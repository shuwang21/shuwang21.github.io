Q&A Tips

  • Write down 30-50 questions
  • Categorize them, like Related work, limitations, and so on
  • The best answer is less than 30 seconds
  • Map the hard question to the question you prepared
  • Never say "I do not know".
  • For defense, defend everything including every number/examples(including why you evaluated xx amount of benchmarks).
  • No fake answer, treat the Professor seriously
  • Repeat the question first.
  • Never answer a question saying "no, we don't do that". Could answer "fine, you could do that. That feature is orthogonal to what we propose."

Conference Presentation Tips

  • Begin: Thanks for the introduction.
  • End: Thanks for all your attention. Now, I am happy to answer any questions.
  • Give a one sentence: the take-away information
  • The outline should be after giving the motifivation part
